Building and Architecture

The Building and Architecture department focuses on the art and science of constructing structures within Minecraft. Students in this department learn about architectural design, structural integrity, and aesthetic principles to create impressive buildings and landscapes. From towering skyscrapers to intricate castles, students explore various building styles and techniques to bring their creative visions to life.

Courses in this department cover topics such as:

Village Planning
Planning Houses Planning Roads and Pathways Planning Community Spaces

Smart Redstone Cities
Planning Railway Systems Planning Street Lighting Planning Security Systems

Biome-Based Urban Planning
Planning Desert Cities Planning Ocean Cities Planning Snow Cities

Redstone Engineering

The Redstone Engineering department focuses on the mechanics and applications of redstone circuitry in Minecraft. Students in this department learn how to create complex redstone contraptions, from simple doors and traps to intricate automated systems. The curriculum covers topics such as logic gates, timing circuits, and redstone-powered machines, allowing students to harness the power of redstone to enhance their builds and gameplay.
